    How I Work

    I will take the time that is needed to get to you and your project. While we can meet online or offline, I prefer to kick start a new project with a personal meeting. Meet me in my office in downtown Miami or I will come to you as long as you are in the South Florida area. After your initial meeting, you will receive a project proposal within less than 48 hours. The project proposal will contain the project timeline as well as the full quote. .
